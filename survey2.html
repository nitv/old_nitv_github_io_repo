iPhone App Survey

Welcome to the iPhone Survey App. You will be taken to the actual app once you click on the 'Begin' button below.

In the app you will be shown a set of images and asked a few questions after you've seen those images.

  • Once you press the 'Next' button below, you will be shown an a screen grab of an iPhone app with an ad banner.
  • Observe that image, and then click 'Next' on that page.
  • You will then see only the screen grab of the app (i.e. without the ad).
  • Then, when you click 'Next' again, you will be asked a set of questions.
